Privacy Policy for NH GOODS

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

NH QUALITY GOODS LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the NH GOODS mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Phone number, name, shop name, and shop address provided during registration.
  • Order Information: Products ordered, order history, and payment preferences.
  • Camera and Photos: We access your camera only when you use the "Snap & Ask" feature to take product photos for inquiries, or when uploading product images. Photos are only sent with your explicit action.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect location data to determine proximity to your registered shop address for delivery purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill wholesale orders
  • To communicate with you about orders and inquiries
  • To generate invoices and delivery documentation
  • To improve our services and product offerings

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only used for providing our wholesale services to you.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted authentication and secure data storage.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Children's Privacy

NH GOODS is a business-to-business application. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the application.
