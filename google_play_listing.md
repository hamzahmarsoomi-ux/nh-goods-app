## Full Description (4000 characters max)
NH GOODS is the official wholesale ordering app for NH QUALITY GOODS LLC (Brand: Vision & Faith). Designed exclusively for registered wholesale customers, this app streamlines your entire ordering process from browsing products to receiving invoices.

KEY FEATURES:

PRODUCT CATALOG
- Browse our complete wholesale product catalog
- View wholesale pricing and product details
- Filter by categories: Snacks, Beverages, Dairy, Grocery, Frozen, Household, and more
- Search products quickly and easily

EASY ORDERING
- Add products to your cart with one tap
- Review your order before checkout
- Track your order status in real-time
- View order history

FLASH DEALS
- Access exclusive daily deals and promotions
- Save more on bulk orders

DIGITAL INVOICING
- Receive professional digital invoices
- View and download invoices as PDF
- Sequential invoice numbering for easy tracking

IN-APP MESSAGING
- Direct communication with NH GOODS team
- Share photos and documents
- Get quick responses to your inquiries

SNAP & ASK
- Take a photo of any product
- Send it to us for pricing and availability
- Quick and easy product inquiries

MULTI-LANGUAGE SUPPORT
- English
- Spanish (Español)
- Urdu (اردو)
- Hindi (हिंदी)
- Nepali (नेपाली)
- Chinese (中文)

SECURE & PRIVATE
- Private access for registered customers only
- Secure phone number + PIN authentication
- Your data is protected

NH QUALITY GOODS LLC serves wholesale customers with quality products at competitive prices. Download NH GOODS today and simplify your wholesale ordering experience!
